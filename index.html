Simon Steyskal

Since 2014, I have been involved in a joint research project between TU Vienna and Siemens Austria where at the latter I'm also employed as a research scientist and am conducting a PhD under the supervision of Prof. Gerti Kappel. Furthermore, I also work as a project assistant at the Vienna University of Economics and Business doing a PhD under the supervision of Prof. Axel Polleres. Prior to that I have received a master's degree in Software Engineering & Internet Computing in November 2013 (Thesis: Towards an Alternative Approach for Combining Ontology Matchers), another master's degree in Information and Knowledge Management in January 2015 (Thesis: Defining an Actor Ontology for Increasing Energy Efficiency and User Comfort in Smart Homes), and a bachelor's degree in Business Informatics in June 2011, all from TU Vienna.
